Oakwood Cinema Reviews

All reviews are done by seven Oakwood Secondary School students

Why we do it

We actively participate in watching and appreciating film and we have decided to put our own views on movies for you, the viewer to see. This group started when we applied to our schools film class. All of us didn't get in so we started a group in where we watch movies three from different time periods and give our takes on them. We started this website to show our views on movies that we have collectively watched together.

How we review:

We individually give a score of 100% and average from all of us. In our review section we calloborate and write the review together expressing individual ideas and thoughts on the movie.
